M'LORDS' GRUEL

"For when a real meal wont cut it"

"Just enough water"

there are many different variations to this recipe depending upon your social status

NOBILITY

reserved only for those of the highest social standing

you will need to tax the following items

  • organically grown Barley or Oats
  • Tenderloin from the best bull
  • purified water from a mountain stream
  • exotic herbs from the Far-East
  • freshly picked mushrooms of the highest caliber
  • beef broth
  • eggs from the royal flock

Now that you have gotten someone else to get you the ingredients, have your chef prepare it in this order to bring out the maximum flavor

  1. bring the water to a slight boil
  2. add the oats
  3. wait ten minutes then add the beef broth
  4. next comes the herbs and mushrooms after finely dicing them
  5. add the tenderloin(which should have already been cooked)
  6. finally, add the eggs to thicken the soup

allow the eggs to cook in the soup for best flavor

DO NOT OVERCOOK

ALLOW TO COOL DOWN TO ROOM TEMPERATURE BEFORE SERVING AS TO NOT BURN YOUR LORDS MOUTH (you dont want to know what happened to the last chef who served the food too hot!)

MERCANTILE

you will need to ask permision, but grab these items fronm the store house

  • milk
  • water
  • oats
  • herbs
  • pork

PEASANT

and finally a meal fit for the hardest workers in the kingdom!

from your daily rations you will need

  • semi-clean water from a ditch
  • unrotten barley
  • grass or leaves

  1. bring the water to a boil in whatever container you have available
  2. next add the barley
  3. finally add the vegetation for flavor

allow the water to come to a bubbling boil to destroy any evil magic placed upon the grains

you may ask your lord for increased rations, about there will be penance to pay on the 'morrow...
